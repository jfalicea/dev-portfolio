About Me

Welcome to my little slice of the internet! My name is Jonathan Alicea, and I am web developer. I started my career as a Financial Institution Regulator with the Florida Office of Financial Regulation. After leaving public section, I joined an up and coming IT consulting firm, Focus Audits, LLC, where I was tasked with implementing and maintaining the Information Security Programs for Financial Institutions with Regulatory Enforcement Actions. I managed to combine my information security and auditing experiences to develop the firm’s IT Audit Program, which is based upon FFIEC guidelines and leading IT and IS Frameworks. While working for Focus Audits, I was immersed in new technology questions and puzzles each day. I finally got to the point where learning about technology in my free time wasn't enough, so I enrolled in a Software Development Bootcamp. While software development is way different than being an auditor, I think it's safe to say I think I finally found a place where I belong.
